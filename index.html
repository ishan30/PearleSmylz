Dr Nachiket Shah

MDS (PedoDentistry) Gold Medalist - Manipal Univesity.

Graduated from the Nair hospital dental college, Mumbai in 2010. He completed his masters from Manipal college of dental sciences, Mangalore in pedodontics and preventive dentistry in 2014 as a gold medalist. He has a pediatric practice in the western suburb (Kandivali) in Mumbai since July 2014 and is attached to various clinics in the western suburbs. Performs general anesthesia as per need at a nearby hospital under guided supervision. He is a trained professional for use of nitrous oxide sedation for safe and comfortable treatment. Dr Nachiket is passionate about always enhancing his knowledge and keeping up with the new and sound treatment methodology of the age.

He has various national publications to his credit and has presented various scientific papers at national conferences during his course as a student. His field of interest lies in early orthodontic intervention and preventive dental care for the child. Looking forward to healthy little smiles.
